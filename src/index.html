Overview

Myraa Lens transforms any standard camera into a smart camera, integrating advanced AI and mixed reality to enhance manufacturing processes. It provides real-time monitoring, automates quality checks, issues immediate alerts for safety hazards, and enables remote inspections using drones and cameras. With Myraa Lens, you can achieve higher efficiency, ensure consistent quality, and maintain comprehensive audit trails, all while reducing operational costs and enhancing workplace safety.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Monitoring
  • Quality Check Automation
  • Warning and Emergency Alerts
  • Real-Time Hazard Alarms
  • Remote Accessibility
  • Clear Audits
