Industries and Applications

Oil and Gas Industry

Leak Detection

Myraa Lens uses AI to monitor pipelines and equipment for leaks in real time, providing early warnings to prevent environmental damage and ensure operational safety.

Equipment Monitoring

Continuous oversight of critical equipment detects signs of wear and tear or malfunction, allowing for timely maintenance and reducing the risk of equipment failure.

Safety and Compliance

Real-time alerts for potential hazards like gas leaks or equipment malfunctions enhance safety and maintain regulatory compliance.

Remote Accessibility

Drones and mobile cameras equipped with Myraa Lens inspect hard-to-reach or hazardous areas, reducing the need for personnel exposure to danger.

Process Optimization

Myraa Lens offers a comprehensive view of all activities, helping managers identify constrictions and optimize processes for better efficiency.

Process Twin for Pipeline Management

Uses digital twin technology to simulate and manage pipeline operations.

Aerospace Industry

Screw Tightness Detection

Ensures the integrity and safety of aircraft by checking the tightness of screws.

Digital Twin for Aircraft Maintenance

Simulates aircraft systems for predictive maintenance and optimization.

Foreign Object Detection

Identifies foreign objects in assembly areas to prevent potential damage and ensure safety.

Steel Industry

Motor Repair Logistics

Monitors the logistics of motor repairs.

Process Twin for Manufacturing Optimization

Enhances manufacturing processes using digital twin simulations.

Object Recognition

Recognizes different shapes of bars for better inventory management.

Construction Industry

Site Monitoring

Process Twin for Project Management

Simulates construction processes for better planning and management.

Quality Inspections

Automates construction quality inspection, ensuring compliance with standards.

Logistics and Supply Chain

Inventory Management

Uses AI to track and manage inventory, ensuring optimal stock levels.

Process Twin for Supply Chain Optimization

Shipment Monitoring

Monitors the status and condition of shipments in real time.

Healthcare Industry

Equipment Monitoring

Myraa Lens continuously monitors medical equipment to ensure it functions correctly and safely. Any deviations or potential failures are detected in real-time, allowing immediate corrective actions.

Detection of Visible Health Conditions

AI is used to monitor patients' visible health conditions, providing real-time alerts for any concerning changes, thus enhancing the quality of patient care and early intervention.

Safety Alerts

Provides real-time alerts for any safety issues within healthcare facilities, such as equipment malfunctions or hazardous conditions, ensuring prompt responses to prevent accidents and ensure patient safety.

Human Twin for Patient Monitoring

Simulates patient health data to provide a comprehensive view of patient conditions, enabling improved care and monitoring through predictive analytics.

Operational Efficiency

By creating a digital twin of hospital operations, Myraa Lens provides a comprehensive overview of workflows, helping to identify bottlenecks, optimize resource allocation, and streamline processes.

Remote Accessibility

Allows for remote monitoring and diagnostics using mobile cameras and wearables, reducing the need for physical presence in potentially hazardous or hard-to-reach areas within the facility.

Compliance and Audits

Ensures all operations are compliant with healthcare regulations by maintaining detailed and tamper-proof audit trails, facilitating easier and more accurate regulatory compliance.

Predictive Maintenance

Utilizes predictive analytics to foresee equipment maintenance needs before failures occur, ensuring continuous operation and reducing downtime.

Provides real-time monitoring of utility sites to ensure safety and compliance.

Vessel Inspection

Process Twin for Fleet Management
